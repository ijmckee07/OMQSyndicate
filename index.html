More Information

This is an awesome little aircraft. Easy to fly no-frills fun plane. To meet all the CASA and Insurance requirements, pilots must present:
A current AU pilots license (or OS equivalent)
A current medical certificate
Have at least 5 hours on type (an instructor can be organised to obtain proficiency)
Have at least 100 hours total time (insurance requirement)

Insurance requires that all prospective hirers undertake a short flight check with an owner which consists of 3 circuits, a normal landing, flapless, and glide approach. Estimate maybe 0.3 logbook hours at hire rates with a 0.2 fixed fee at owners rates.

The hire rate is based on TACHO, wet hire, inc GST. When compared with other hire rates, it would equate to $232 elsewhere. The syndicate operates as a non-profit with all funds poured back into safety and maintenance.

Syndicate Ownership

From time to time syndicate positions become available. Being a syndicate owner entitles priority use of the aircraft, 30% reduced hire rate, and ability to take the aircraft for extended periods.

The syndicate works on a non-profit and all funds are poured back into maintenace, upgrades, and the safe upkeep of the aircraft. All syndicate owners get the benefit of any profit by maintaining low cost flying hours and no upkeep costs. The aircraft will be upgraded regularly with two LAMEs and an avionics design engineer involved in the syndicate.

There is no flying school attached to this aircraft and share holders are not asked for any monthly, quartly, bi-annual, or annual fees. As long as the aircraft keeps flying, the only outlay is the initial buy-in, which is recovered if you ever sell your share, but why would you! The initial buy in period is one year to maintain stability for the syndicate.

Contact the syndicate administration at Ownership Enquiry for more information about the benefits of owning an aviation asset.

The Caboolture Aero Club requires all pilots that use the airfiled to be members. Enquire with CAC for membership details. This is a great deal considering there are no landing fees, and the clubhouse facilities that are included.
