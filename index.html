More Information

An awesome little aircraft. Easy to fly hourbuilder. To meet all the CASA and Insurance requirements, pilots must present:
A current AU pilots license
A current medical certificate
A current BFR
Have at least 100 hours total time (insurance requirement)

Insurance requires that all prospective hirers undertake a short check flight with an owner.

The hire rate is based on TACHO, wet hire, inc GST.

The syndicate operates as a non-profit with all funds poured back into safety and maintenance.

Syndicate Ownership

Syndicate shares are currently available. A syndicate share holder is entitled to priority use of the aircraft, 33% reduced hire rate, and ability to take the aircraft for extended periods.

The syndicate is a non-profit with all funds put toward maintenace, upgrades, and the safe upkeep of the aircraft.

Share holders are not asked for any monthly, quartly, bi-annual, or annual fees, as long as the aircraft keeps flying, with the only outlay being the initial buy-in. The initial buy in period is one year to maintain stability for the syndicate.

Contact the syndicate administration at -Ownership Enquiry - for more information about the benefits of owning an aviation asset.

The Caboolture Aero Club requires all pilots that regularly use the airfiled to be members. Enquire with CAC for membership details.
